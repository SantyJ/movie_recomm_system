3. MATHEMATICAL EXPLANATION OF CALCULATIONS
-------------------------------------------------------
=> MAE calculates:  Sum( |Actual - Predicted| ) / N
=> RMSE calculates: Sqrt( Sum( (Actual - Predicted)^2 ) / N )
Where N is the total number of items in the test set.
An MAE of ~0.8 means our predictions are off by only 0.8 stars on average.
